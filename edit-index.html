F.A.Q

Frequently Asked Questions

New here? view our F.A.Q.

  • What is Cyber World Organization?

    Cyber World Organization is a Non-Profit Organization registered Under Ministry of MSME, Govt. of India via Startup India.

  • What are the CWO's sub-bodies and how do I connect?

    CWO is made up of five divisions, each with its own work domain. which are as follows:

    • Cyber Crime Agency
    • N00B Hackers Community
    • Cyber Salvager
    • Coders Flow AI
    • Cyber Suraksha
  • How do I get an internship under any of these sub-divisions?

    You can visit any of these units as per your interest.

  • Can I work with the internal team?

    Yes, of course. You can apply for volunteer and full-time employment under CWO, and there is a form to be filled out for that here: Apply Here

  • Who is founder of the CWO?

    Mr. Sumit Kumar is Founder and CEO of the whole CWO.

  • Where is your office located in Delhi?

    Currently, Our office is virtually located in New Delhi in North West region.
